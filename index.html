Our Mission

Relaunched in 2025, TEDxTruro strives to become a premier event in the industry, inspiring conversation and growing the business community across Cornwall

What is a TEDx Event

In the spirit of TED's mission, “ideas worth spreading,” the TEDx program helps communities, organisations and individuals produce TED-style events at the local level. TEDx events are planned and coordinated independently, on a community-by-community basis. Each event financially relies on ticket sales and partners to support its running costs and growth.

We handpick each of our speakers for their expertise and innovative ideas, ensuring that our guests are exposed to the latest trends and thought-provoking insights.
