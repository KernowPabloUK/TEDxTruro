About

Experience the best of TEDxTruro

TedXTruro is a local offshoot of the globally recognised Tedx conference platform. TEDx Talks, known for their inspiring short speeches, cover a wide range of topics from science and technology to the arts and global issues. TEDx events bring the spirit of TED Talks to local communities, offering a platform for local innovators and thought leaders to share their ideas with the Truro community.

TEDxTruro events aim to spark conversation and inspire action around ideas that matter. They provide a platform for local voices to be heard on a global stage.

Providing Visibility and Opportunities

TEDx talks are showcased on a variety of platforms, including:

  • The TEDx YouTube channel: This channel features a wide range of TEDx talks from all over the world.
  • The TED website: The TED website also features a selection of TEDx talks, as well as information about the TEDx program.
  • Individual TEDx event websites: Each TEDx event has its own website, which typically features videos of the talks from that event.

Join Our Community

At TEDxTruro, we believe in the power of community. We strive to create an inclusive and welcoming environment where everyone can come together to celebrate and have fun. Join our community today and experience the magic of TEDxTruro for yourself!
